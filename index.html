Elliot Gross

I work on political campaigns, most recently, Alex Bores for Congress. My passions include AI safety, animal welfare, progressive politics, philosophy, reading fiction, writing fiction, bluegrass and Argentine rock music, backpacking, mountain biking, eating (not cooking), the New York Knicks basketball team, and long walks on short beaches.

Please send me an email and talk to me about any of the things on this list, or any of the things not on this list that you are still ontologically committed to.

I’m originally from Kentucky, but I’m currently based in NYC. In past lives, I’ve fought wildfires in the west, farmed in Ecuador, dabbled in legal internships, and been a philosophy student at Columbia University.
